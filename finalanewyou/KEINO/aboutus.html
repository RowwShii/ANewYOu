About Us

At A new You, we are dedicated to supporting individuals and communities on their journey towards emotional well-being and mental clarity. Our approach is grounded in compassion and a deep commitment to accessible care.

Our History

Founded in 2025 by a group of college students and advocates, Thrive began as a small community initiative focused on providing therapy to underserved populations. Over the years, we've expanded into a multidisciplinary organization offering therapy, psychiatric care, community outreach, and educational programs across multiple locations.
